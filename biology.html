lipids (least mass)

lipids are generally insoluble in water, they are non polar

  1. high proportion of nonpolar c-h bonds.
  2. not polymers, not covalently bonded
  3. weak but additive van der waals, hydrophobic
  4. fats, oils, waxs, some vitamins and hormones.
